AngelSlim is a comprehensive large model compression toolkit developed by Tencent Hunyuan AI Infra. It unifies quantization, speculative decoding, sparse attention, token pruning, and distillation into a pipeline from algorithm research to industrial-scale deployment. The toolkit is open sourced at [Tencent/AngelSlim](https://github.com/tencent/AngelSlim).
